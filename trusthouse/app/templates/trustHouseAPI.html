Trust House APIs

Backend services for users and developers to collaborate with.


Who's It For? What's The Purpose?

The Trust House API is open source, meaning its available for everyone to access - for free!

From private buisnesses, to landlords, letting agencies, the local authority and even the public, anyone can access the data from Trust House by following the steps provided below.

Even though the reviews and the updates made to the map are anonymous, Trust House still wanted to provide a level of transparency - so we made the web app open source and also enabled a number of backend services.

These backend services can be used by the public, and also fellow developers to use the Trust House data within their own applications.

Upload New Address

For this API users can create a new address simply by entering the details in the web browser. Although it is accessible for the public, primarily this is set up for businesses and private landlords who may want to include their property into the Trust House database.
The user will recieve a response as a JSON format, letting them know if their upload was successful or not.

Type in web browser:
https://trusthouse.onrender.com/api/new/address/address_door_num,address_street_name,address_location,address_postcode

Example:
https://trusthouse.onrender.com/api/new/address/4A,Alfred Street,Blackpool,FY1 4HL


Upload Business To Map

The API allow users who are business owners to upload their services on the Trust House map. This means anyone who has a buisness can upload it to the map in the hope to get more traffic!
Users need to be aware that there are only limited amount of characters allowed for each section of every entry.

For the category entry, users are advised to stick to one word or a short phrase as only 15 characters are allowed (maxium).
Trust House hopes that as this site grows, more users will use this as a utility to advertise their business.

The user will recieve a response as a JSON format, letting them know if their upload was successful or not.

Type in web browser:
https://trusthouse.onrender.com/api/new/buisness/buisness_name,category,contact,door_num,streetname,location,postcode

Example:
https://trusthouse.onrender.com/api/new/business/The Lounge,bar & restuarant85,Bootham Crescent,Ringford,DG7 8RT


Upload Incident To Map

Trust House wanted to provide a level of trust within the community for our users, so we created a way for users to report any incident or emergencies that may happen to occur in or near your area.
Incidents can be reported directly from teh web browser which will then create a marker on the Trust House map.

Trust House must stress to always call the emergency services first if you feel you or anyone else may be in danger!

The incident reported by the user can then be viewed by anyone who has access to the website. In the future Trust House plans to work closely with local authorities where each report will be automatically sent to the emergency services. In future updates a bot could be craeted to send a Twitter post of each incident, linking it to the emergency services.

For the category entry, users are advised to stick to one word or a short phrase as only 15 characters are allowed (maxium).

For the description entry, users are advised to keep 'keep it short' - to one sentance, or two short sentances - as only 40 characters are allowed (maximu).

The user will recieve a response as a JSON format, letting them know if their upload was successful or not.

Type in web browser:
https://trusthouse.onrender.com/api/report/incident/description,door_num,streetname,location,postcode

Example:
https://trusthouse.onrender.com/api/report/Street fight,Two people started arguing now their fighting right outside my house!,96,Windsor Road,Bradford,BD75 0PB
